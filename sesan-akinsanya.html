Our Founder

"Belonging to these other professional associations gives me a deeper insight into understanding and providing solutions to issues facing business clients. As a commercial lawyer, it gives me unique and tremendous advantage to see things from client's perspective".

I am Sesan Akinsanya, the founder of BonitasLaw and it is my pleasure to be given the opportunity to provide you with our service. You are the reason for our existence and in line with our core values of Client-centeredness, Excellence and Success, we are committed to providing you with excellent service to ensure that you achieve your goals for contacting us. We have the competence and the character to deliver on this promise.

I am a multi-skilled commercial professional, as I'm not just a lawyer, I belong to some other professional bodies in the commercial realm - I'm a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators, a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and a Member of the Chartered Institute of Purchasing and Supplies. Belonging to these other professional associations gives me a deeper insight into understanding and providing solutions to issues facing business clients. As a commercial lawyer, it gives me unique and tremendous advantage to see things from client's perspective. I am passionate about providing optimum legal solutions to business challenges and helping others to realize their dreams.

I am a dual qualified lawyer as I am licensed to practice law both in Nigeria and Canada. I have been practicing law since I was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1985. My experience is almost equally divided between practicing as external lawyer and being employed as in-house counsel. As part of my in-house career, I spent 19 years in various positions with Shell Petroleum. I possess the professional qualifications and experience to provide quality service in the following areas, among others:

  • Corporate Commercial Law
  • Oil and Gas
  • Corporate Governance
  • Contracting and Procurement
  • Compliance Management
  • Entertainment Law
  • Litigation, Arbitration and ADR

Education
University of Lagos

Professional Membership

  • Law Society of Ontario
  • Nigerian Bar Association
  • Chartered Institute of Arbitrators
  • Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators
  • Chartered Institute of Purchasing and Supplies
  • Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria

I look forward to providing you with the needed professional support towards achieving your goals.
